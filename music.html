Timothy Lew is a San Francisco native who became fascinated with electronic music after beginning college at USC. Initially starting off with his music interests by performing DJ sets at various events around USC as his alias Lewcid, Timothy decided he wanted to get more involved with actual music production shortly after graduating and took on his new "after-work" name: LEWT.
